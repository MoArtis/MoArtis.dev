DreadOut Remastered Collection is putting the two PC-only original games, DreadOut (2014) and Keepers of the dark (2016) in one improved package for consoles.

I took part in the herculean effort to port the Unity 4 codebase to Unity 2022. As it was expected, a lot of core systems had to be redone due to compatibility issues between these two versions and on the necessities of a console port.

That being said, it was a great experience and my second successful console port effort (after Swoon).
