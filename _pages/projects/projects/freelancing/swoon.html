Swoon! Earth Escape is a classic 2D platformer with a 3D presentation where you play as Swoon, an alien who land crashed on a post-apocalyptic Earth. In order to escape you have to collect all the mechanical parts of your spaceship placed in each levels.

I assisted the solo developer of Swoon! throughout the end of its development. I rewrote the Character controller, helped with translation system and assisted the porting process.

I'm glad to have been able to help a fellow indie developer. Hopefully this humble first game might be his first step toward building a successful game studio.
