Automatic System AR is an app created to help that company's clients to pre-visualize the placement of their turnstile and barrier products through augmented reality.

I was hired on the project as a subcontractor by the app developer, Oniki agency. They had trouble with Unity's latest UI system called UI Toolkit. Luckily for them, I spent the previous months working with this feature for one of my tools, ClipSync.

Thanks to my support during the final push, they were able to deliver the milestone on time.

Helping a team in difficulty is always the most enjoyable contracts as the stakes are much higher, and the impact of my contributions is immediate.
